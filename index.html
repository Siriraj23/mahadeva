BPT

BPT rice, also known as Sona Masoori, is a lightweight, aromatic, medium-grain rice variety, and the full form of BPT is "Bangaru Thope" in Telugu, which translates to "Golden Crop".

HMT

HMT rice, a popular paddy variety, stands for Hindustan Machine Tools and was developed by Dadaji Ramji Khobragade, an experimental farmer from Chandrapur, Maharashtra.

RNR

RNR rice, also known as Telangana Sona (RNR 15048), is a new rice variety developed by Professor Jayashankar Telangana State Agricultural University (PJTSAU) with a low glycemic index, making it suitable for diabetic and weight loss management.

JAY SREE RAM

"Jai Shree Ram" rice, a popular brand of non-basmati rice, is a reference to the Hindu deity Lord Ram, meaning "Victory to Lord Ram" or "Hail Lord Ram".
